Migration story
---------------
The pass-1 migration scaffold + the pass-2.5 hive-identity extension
(`encrypted_content::migration::*` + `scripts/migrate-dna.ts`)
together activate on the pass-2 DNA-hash change. Hosts running pass-1
see their entries' `get_migration_marker` reader return V1 markers
pointing at pass-2 entry hashes once authors have published
forward-pointer markers; pass-2-aware hosts see V1+V2 markers via
`get_migration_marker_v2`, with V2 markers additionally carrying the
new HiveGenesis hash so members discover hive-identity moves.

(pass-3-aware; not currently produced by export but accepted on
